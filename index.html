School Facilities

At Zanzibar Morning Star Academy(ZAMOSA), we are dedicated to providing a supportive, well-equipped, and modern environment to help our students thrive academically, creatively, and socially. Our state-of-the-art facilities are designed to foster learning, encourage collaboration, and promote the well-being of all students.

School Bus

At ZAMOSA, we prioritize the safety and convenience of our students, which is why we offer a reliable and secure school bus service. Our buses are designed to provide comfortable and timely transportation for students, ensuring they arrive at school and return home safely every day.

Playground

At ZAMOSA, we believe that play is an essential part of a child’s growth and development. Our playgrounds provide a safe, fun, and stimulating environment where students can enjoy physical activity, build friendships, and develop important life skills. Designed with a focus on safety and creativity, our playground facilities cater to students of all ages.

Healthy Canteen

At ZAMOSA, we understand that good nutrition fuels learning and growth. Our Healthy Canteen offers a variety of wholesome and delicious meal options, providing students with the nourishment they need to stay energized and focused throughout the day. With an emphasis on fresh, balanced meals, we encourage healthy eating habits that support the overall well-being of our students.

Positive Learning

At ZAMOSA, we are committed to creating a positive, supportive, and inclusive learning environment where every student feels valued and empowered to reach their full potential. Our approach to education goes beyond academics, fostering a space that nurtures personal growth, creativity, and resilience.

Learn More About Our Work And Our Cultural Activities

At ZAMOSA, education extends beyond the classroom. We believe that cultural activities play a vital role in a well-rounded education, enriching students’ lives and helping them develop a deeper understanding of the world around them. Our commitment to holistic development is reflected in our diverse programs that celebrate creativity, community, and cultural awareness.

Our dedicated faculty and staff work tirelessly to provide a challenging and innovative curriculum that caters to the unique talents of each student. We emphasize critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration in every subject, creating an environment where students are encouraged to excel academically and personally.

Become A Teacher

At ZAMOSA, we are passionate about creating a positive, inspiring, and supportive environment for students to thrive—and this starts with exceptional teachers. We are looking for educators who are dedicated, innovative, and driven to make a difference. Join our team to help shape the future of young minds while advancing your own career in a collaborative and fulfilling community.
